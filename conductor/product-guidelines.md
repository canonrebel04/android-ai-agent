# Product Guidelines

## Voice and Tone
The application speaks with a **Concise & Technical** voice. Communication should be precise, data-driven, and terminal-oriented. Avoid unnecessary fluff, conversational filler, or excessive pleasantries. The goal is to provide maximum signal with minimum noise.

## User Experience (UX) Philosophy
We adhere to a **Minimalist & Clean** UX philosophy. The interface should focus entirely on the content—the chat and the system outputs. Controls, settings, and secondary information should be tucked away until needed, ensuring a distraction-free environment for the power user.

## Visual Aesthetic
The app uses a **Modern Material3** aesthetic. This combines the latest Android design standards with a clean, adaptive, and highly readable look. While the core is a powerful terminal-style agent, the interface remains approachable, modern, and perfectly integrated with the Android OS.

## Motion and Animation
Interaction should feel **Subtle & Snappy**. Motion is used sparingly to provide meaningful feedback for user actions without slowing down the workflow. Transitions should be short and purposeful, reinforcing the feeling of a high-performance, responsive tool.
